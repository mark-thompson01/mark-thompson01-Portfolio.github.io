CompTIA Security+ Certification

About

The CompTIA Security+ certification validates the skills required to perform core security functions and pursue a career in cybersecurity.

My Experience

Achieving the Security+ certification allowed me to master critical security principles, including risk management, cryptography, and secure network design. It laid the groundwork for my transition into cybersecurity roles.

I began preparing for the CompTIA Security+ certification as I transitioned from a help desk role into my first IT Department opportunity as a Desktop Support technician. This new role gave me the perfect environment to start applying security fundamentals in a practical way.

Starting with the basics, I was driven to implement as much security as possible. I focused on:

  • Securing device configurations across computers and systems
  • Changing default credentials and disabling unnecessary services
  • Enabling encryption where applicable to protect sensitive data
  • Setting up multi-factor authentication (MFA) to enhance user access security

Earning the Security+ certification was a pivotal moment for me. It opened my eyes to the diversity within cybersecurity, introducing me to the wide range of roles and opportunities the industry offers.
